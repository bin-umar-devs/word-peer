Privacy Policy for Word Puzzle

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Word Puzzle ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience while using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Word Puzzle app.

2. Information We Collect

Word Puzzle is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

2.1 Information Stored Locally

  • Game Statistics: Your game scores, win/loss records, streaks, and achievements
  • App Preferences: Your theme selection (dark/light mode) and sound settings
  • Progress Data: Information about puzzles you've completed

Important: All this data is stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of this information on our servers.

2.2 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Any other personal data

3. How We Use Your Information

The locally stored information is used solely to:

  • Save your game progress and statistics
  • Remember your app preferences (theme, sound settings)
  • Display your achievements and scores
  • Provide a personalized gaming experience

Since all data is stored locally on your device, we have no access to this information.

4. Data Storage and Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. We do not transmit any data to external servers. Your data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security features.

5. Third-Party Services

Word Puzzle does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app functions completely offline and does not communicate with external servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Word Puzzle is suitable for all ages, including children. Since we do not collect any personal information, there are no privacy concerns regarding children's data. Parents can feel confident that their children's privacy is protected while using our app.

7. Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data. To delete all game data and preferences:

  • Open the app and go to Settings
  • Tap "Reset Statistics" to clear all game data
  • Alternatively, uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data

8. Permissions

Word Puzzle requires minimal permissions:

  • Storage: To save your game progress and preferences locally on your device

We do not request any other permissions such as camera, location, contacts, or microphone access.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app and on this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates. Your continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

Since Word Puzzle does not collect or transmit any personal data, there are no international data transfer concerns. The app can be used safely from any country.

11. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (access, correction, deletion) don't apply. However, you have complete control over your locally stored game data and can delete it at any time through the app settings or by uninstalling the app.

12. No Advertising

Word Puzzle is completely ad-free. We do not display advertisements or use any ad networks that might collect user data.
